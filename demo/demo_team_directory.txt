## Escalation guide
- Order blocked for more than two business days: contact Elena Rossi.
- Invoice overdue, disputed, partially paid, or awaiting bank confirmation: contact Marco Bianchi.
- Customer repeatedly reports poor support response: contact Sofia Conti.
- API sync, webhook, or access issue: contact Daniel Weber.
- Duplicate or inconsistent business records: contact Priya Shah.
- Picking, packing, stock, or carrier handoff issue: contact Thomas Reed.
- Employee onboarding, equipment, or office access request: contact Amina Haddad.
- Product roadmap or release-status question: contact Luca Moretti.

## Internal process notes
- Employees should avoid sharing real credentials through support tickets or chat systems.
- Demo contacts use the example.invalid domain and cannot receive real email.
- If a question asks for private salary, medical, disciplinary, or personal identity details, the chatbot should say the answer is not available in the uploaded documents.
- If multiple employees could handle a request, the chatbot should identify the most directly responsible employee and mention the department.
- If a question asks for a real phone number, bank account, password, access token, or personal address, the chatbot should refuse or fall back because this demo document does not contain that information.
